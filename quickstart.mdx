HashPilot requires no account, no sign-up, and no configuration. You can get a full claim analysis in under two minutes. This guide walks you through the complete flow from opening the terminal to exploring the rest of the tools after your first verdict.

<Tip>
  You don't need a wallet to try HashPilot. The demo wallet lets you explore every feature of the Claim Advisor immediately — no connection required.
</Tip>

## Get your first verdict

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the terminal">
    Navigate to [hashpilot-taupe.vercel.app](https://hashpilot-taupe.vercel.app) in your browser. You'll land on the HashPilot home screen — a retro-terminal interface with a wallet input field at the center.

    No account is required. The terminal is publicly accessible and works immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your wallet or paste an address">
    You have three options at this step:

    **Option A — Connect via RainbowKit (recommended for your own wallet)**

    Click **Connect Wallet** in the top navigation bar. RainbowKit will display the connection modal with all supported wallet options. Select your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, and others are supported), approve the connection, and your address will automatically populate the input field.

    **Option B — Paste an address directly**

    If you don't want to connect a wallet, paste any valid Avalanche wallet address (format: `0x...`, 42 characters) into the input field. This works for any address — your own, a friend's, or any public wallet.

    **Option C — Use the demo wallet**

    Click the **Use demo wallet** link below the input field. This loads a pre-configured demo address:

    ```text
    0x8f9a59b6574f9bf10398863673c6c06a6c0735d9
    ```

    The demo wallet has real simulated HashCash data (450.25 hCASH pending, 15.40 hCASH estimated claim fee) and will show you a live CLAIM NOW verdict so you can explore the full interface.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run the analysis">
    Click **Analyze My Wallet** (on the home screen) or **Execute Analysis** (on the Claim Advisor page). The terminal will display a loading indicator while it fetches your on-chain HashCash data.

    Analysis typically completes in 1–2 seconds. HashPilot reads the following data for your address:

    - Pending hCASH rewards accumulated since your last claim
    - Estimated claim fee at current network conditions
    - Your last claim timestamp and current strategy mode
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read your verdict">
    The Claim Advisor delivers one of two verdicts:

    | Verdict | Condition | What it means |
    |---|---|---|
    | **CLAIM NOW** | Pending rewards ≥ 2× the claim fee | Your rewards are large enough that claiming now is net-positive after fees. |
    | **WAIT** | Pending rewards < 2× the claim fee | Claiming now would consume too large a share of your rewards in gas. Wait for more accumulation or a gas dip. |

    The 2× threshold is the efficiency floor — it ensures that after paying the claim fee, you retain at least 50% of the rewards you claim. Claiming below this threshold costs more in relative gas than it's worth.

    Below the verdict, the **Technical Parameters** panel shows the raw numbers:

    - **Pending Rewards** — your accumulated hCASH balance
    - **Estimated Claim Fee** — the current gas cost in hCASH terms
    - **Breakeven Threshold** — the minimum rewards needed for a net-positive claim (2× the fee)

    The **Intelligent Analysis** section below the verdict panel contains Claude AI's plain-language reasoning, including your rewards-to-fee ratio and a specific recommendation based on current network conditions.

    <Warning>
      Gas fee spikes can flip a CLAIM NOW verdict to WAIT within minutes. If you're on the borderline, check back after network congestion clears before acting.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the other tools">
    After reviewing your verdict, three quick-action buttons appear on the verdict card:

    - **Claim Now** — a direct reminder prompt (HashPilot itself does not initiate the claim; you do this in the Club HashCash interface)
    - **Set Alert** — configure a notification for when conditions improve
    - **View Full Ops Snapshot** — open the tactical dashboard for live price feeds and network status

    From the navigation bar, you can also jump directly to:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Oracle" icon="chart-line" href="/tools/oracle">
        Model your earnings across Bear, Base, and Bull scenarios over 30, 90, and 180 days.
      </Card>
      <Card title="FOMO Machine" icon="triangle-exclamation" href="/tools/fomo-machine">
        Calculate the hCASH cost of your past suboptimal decisions.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Miner Archetypes" icon="user-shield" href="/tools/miner-archetypes">
        Take the 6-question assessment to find your mining persona and get a strategy playbook.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Dashboard" icon="gauge" href="/dashboard/overview">
        Live hCASH DEX price, AVAX price, and claim cycle progress.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Demo mode in detail

The demo wallet (`0x8f9a59b6574f9bf10398863673c6c06a6c0735d9`) is a fully functional preview that loads pre-configured data reflecting a realistic mining position:

| Parameter | Demo value |
|---|---|
| Pending rewards | 450.25 hCASH |
| Estimated claim fee | 15.40 hCASH |
| Breakeven threshold | 30.80 hCASH |
| Strategy mode | Aggressive |
| Last claim | 3 days ago |

With 450.25 hCASH pending against a 30.80 hCASH threshold, the demo always yields a **CLAIM NOW** verdict — your rewards represent approximately 29× the efficiency floor, well above the 2× minimum.

Use the demo wallet when you want to explore the UI before connecting your own wallet, or when showing the platform to someone else.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The analysis returns 'No HashCash data found'">
    This means the wallet address you entered has no Club HashCash miner NFTs or no pending rewards detectable on-chain. Verify that you're using an address associated with an active Club HashCash miner. If you just want to explore the interface, use the demo wallet instead.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My connected wallet address didn't populate automatically">
    If you connected via RainbowKit but the address field is empty, try refreshing the page. The wallet sync runs on mount — a hard refresh re-initializes the connection state. If the issue persists, paste your address manually into the input field.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The address format is rejected">
    HashPilot validates that addresses start with `0x` and are exactly 42 characters long. ENS names and shortened addresses are not supported — use the full checksummed hex address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
