Miner Archetypes is HashPilot's identity classification system. It analyzes your decision-making patterns across six behavioral dimensions — claim timing, hCASH usage, miner selection, halving response, primary goal, and network hashrate reaction — and maps them to one of six strategic archetypes. The result is not a personality type. It is an operational profile: a precise characterization of how you mine, what you do well, where you are exposed, and what your next three moves should be.

## The Assessment

The quiz consists of six questions, each with four answer options. There are no trick questions and no right answers — the options map directly to behavioral patterns associated with specific archetypes.

Progress is shown as a step counter (`SCAN 1/6` through `SCAN 6/6`). Questions animate in from the right as you advance. You cannot go back.

### How Scoring Works

Each answer contributes weighted points to one or more archetypes. After all six answers are collected, the system (`calculateArchetype`) sums the weights and returns the archetype with the highest total score. In the event of a tie, the first-encountered maximum wins.

After you answer the final question, a 4.5-second scanning animation plays — a horizontal amber line sweeps the screen three times while status messages appear (*ANALYZING CLAIM PATTERNS… CROSS-REFERENCING STRATEGY MATRIX… CLASSIFICATION CONFIRMED*) — before the reveal screen fades in.

## The Six Archetypes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="⚡ The Compounder" icon="bolt">
    **Color:** #39FF14 (neon green)

    **Tagline:** "Reinvest everything. Dominate over time."

    **Description:** Plays the long game. Every hCASH earned goes back into hashrate. Price today is irrelevant — the goal is to build a mining empire block by block.

    **Strengths:** Network dilution resistance · Compound growth advantage · Halving-resistant strategy

    **Vulnerability:** Illiquid if price drops suddenly. No exit buffer.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Reinvest 90%+ of hCASH earnings into new miners
    2. Upgrade facility tier before next halving
    3. Target miners with best hashrate per hCASH cost
  </Card>
  <Card title="🎯 The Efficiency Hawk" icon="target">
    **Color:** #00D4FF (electric cyan)

    **Tagline:** "Every watt counts. Precision over power."

    **Description:** Obsesses over hashrate-per-watt ratios. Would rather run 6 perfectly optimized USB Nanos than 2 power-hungry Titans. Facility never exceeds 90% power capacity.

    **Strengths:** Low operational cost · Surge-resistant (low claim fees) · Highly predictable earnings

    **Vulnerability:** Slower absolute hashrate growth. Cannot compete in hash wars.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Never fill facility power capacity above 85%
    2. Always choose efficiency rating over raw hashrate
    3. Claim more frequently to avoid large fee exposure
  </Card>
  <Card title="🏦 The Accumulator" icon="piggy-bank">
    **Color:** #F5A623 (amber)

    **Tagline:** "Stack hCASH. Wait for the moment."

    **Description:** Not in this for daily income. Building a position. hCASH stays in the wallet until the price is right. Thinks in cycles, not blocks.

    **Strengths:** Positioned for price appreciation · Patient strategy · Low fee exposure (infrequent claims)

    **Vulnerability:** Vulnerable to emission drops post-halving.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Claim only during Normal rate windows
    2. Hold hCASH rather than reinvesting immediately
    3. Monitor hCASH/AVAX LP for exit opportunities
  </Card>
  <Card title="⏳ The Halving Sniper" icon="clock">
    **Color:** #FF6B35 (orange)

    **Tagline:** "Time the halvings. Extract maximum value."

    **Description:** Entire strategy is built around the halving schedule. Bulk-buys before halvings, claims aggressively after, repositions when the dust settles. The calendar is the weapon.

    **Strengths:** Pre-halving accumulation advantage · Emission timing mastery · Maximum extraction windows

    **Vulnerability:** High variance. Wrong timing = significant losses.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Bulk buy miners 2–3 weeks before each halving
    2. Claim all rewards within 48h post-halving
    3. Sell miners at peak post-halving hashrate
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌊 The Liquidity Player" icon="waves">
    **Color:** #9B59B6 (purple)

    **Tagline:** "Miners are assets. Buy low, mine, flip high."

    **Description:** Treats miner NFTs like a trading portfolio. On the secondary market constantly — buying undervalued miners when others panic, selling into strength, always maintaining optionality.

    **Strengths:** Can profit in any market condition · Not dependent on emissions · Secondary market expertise

    **Vulnerability:** Complex strategy. High cognitive load. Easy to overtrade.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Track miner floor prices on secondary market daily
    2. Buy miners at less than 80% of expected earnings payback
    3. Sell when miner price exceeds 120% payback period
  </Card>
  <Card title="🔰 The Field Recruit" icon="shield">
    **Color:** #7B8FA6 (steel blue)

    **Tagline:** "Learning the ropes. Building the foundation."

    **Description:** Newer to the game, still mapping the terrain. But present, building, and learning something every block. Every expert was once a recruit.

    **Strengths:** No bad habits yet · Low sunk cost · Full optionality

    **Vulnerability:** Susceptible to suboptimal setups. Still learning claim timing.

    **3-Move Playbook:**
    1. Start with USB/entry miners to learn mechanics
    2. Never claim during Surge rate
    3. Track earnings daily for 2 weeks before upgrading
  </Card>

## The Profile Page

After the reveal screen (which shows your archetype name, symbol, tagline, and a button to proceed), the full profile page opens with four sections.

### Core Identity

A prose paragraph — the archetype's `description` field — provides the full behavioral characterization of your mining style in plain language.

### Strengths and Vulnerability

A two-column layout shows:
- **Strengths** as a bullet list with green indicators
- **Vulnerability** as a highlighted warning block in red, naming the single biggest strategic risk your archetype carries

### 3-Move Playbook

A numbered three-column panel labeled **YOUR 3-MOVE PLAYBOOK** displays your archetype's three prioritized action commands. These are sequenced — move 1 is the most immediate priority.

### Archetype Registry

At the bottom of the profile page, a grid shows all six archetypes. Your result is highlighted with an amber ring and a **MY IDENTITY** badge. The other five are rendered with a blur overlay, visible but clearly secondary.

## AI Strategy Roadmap

Below the 3-Move Playbook, click **GET AI STRATEGY ROADMAP** to request a personalized 3-month mining strategy from the HashPilot AI backend (`/api/chat`).

The AI receives your archetype name, tagline, and vulnerability. It returns a specific, actionable roadmap written from the perspective of a high-level mining consultant — encouraging about your strengths, honest about your exposure, and concrete in its month-by-month guidance.

The response streams in and is displayed in a blue-bordered terminal panel as it arrives, so you see the advice build in real time.

<Note>
  The AI Strategy Roadmap requires a configured Anthropic API key. See [API Keys](/setup/api-keys) for setup details. If the backend is unreachable, an error message is displayed in the output panel.
</Note>

## The Exportable Badge Card

Every archetype comes with a shareable identity badge. The badge card is rendered directly in the browser at 4:5 aspect ratio and can be exported as a high-resolution PNG.

The badge includes:
- Your archetype symbol (animated with a subtle pulse)
- Your archetype name in the archetype's accent color with a matching glow shadow
- Your tagline in italic monospace
- Up to three strength tags as small bordered labels
- A "HASHPILOT — MINER PROFILE" header and "IDENTITY VERIFIED // GEN\_LOG\_01" footer

Download by clicking **Download Badge PNG** beneath the card. The file is saved as `archetype-{archetypeId}.png` at 2× pixel ratio.

You can also share directly to X (Twitter) via the **SHARE** button, which pre-populates a tweet with your archetype name, symbol, tagline, and a link to HashPilot.

Use **RETAKE** to restart the assessment from the beginning.
