## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the terminal">
    Navigate to [hashpilot-taupe.vercel.app](https://hashpilot-taupe.vercel.app) — no account required. You can analyze any wallet address immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect or paste your wallet">
    Connect via RainbowKit for the full experience, or paste any Avalanche wallet address (`0x...`) directly into the input field. HashPilot is **read-only** — no transactions are ever initiated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your verdict">
    The [Claim Advisor](/tools/claim-advisor) will immediately analyze your pending hCASH rewards against current claim fees and deliver a clear **CLAIM NOW** or **WAIT** verdict with supporting data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
